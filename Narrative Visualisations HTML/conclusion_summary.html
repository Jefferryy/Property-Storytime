Critical outcomes, Conclusion and Summary insights

Conclusion

This narrative visualisation aimed to explore and bridge the gap in understanding the current Victoria and Melbourne Property market with the primary audience intended to be the general public, however, the scope extends to any individual with an interest to gain a deeper understanding and insight about the current property industry.

Primarily the narrative visualisations created was to foster an understanding with regards to visualising and understanding:

  • Geographically where were all the properties sold throughout Victoria & Melbourne from 2016 - 2018.
  • The number of properties sold in terms of houses throughout Victoria & Melbourne from 1987 - 2018.
  • Comparison between what is a more robust measurement in terms of median house prices and mean house prices.
  • Number of properties sold in terms of units throughout Victoria & Melbourne from 1987 - 2018.
  • Critically understand what is a more robust measurement in terms of median unit prices and mean unit prices.
  • What selling methods and approaches which top real estate agents utilise in the current market and the median return on all properties sold with respective to each method.
  • The current Victoria and Melbourne market with regards to the prices for the properties sold and the methods utilised with respect to the real estate agent.
