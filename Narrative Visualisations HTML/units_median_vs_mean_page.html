Victoria and Melbourne Median Units/Apartments Price Analysis

In terms of residential property statistics for Victoria and Melbourne from 1987 - 2018 whilst Melbourne City had the lowest number of units/apartments sold across every year from 1987 - 2018. However, it had the highest Median Units/Apartments Price growth at 354% from 1987. Thus, this exponential rise in price is primarly attributed to the scarcity in land availability coupled with rising demand for units/apartments.
