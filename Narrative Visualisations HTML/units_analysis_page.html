Victoria and Melbournes number of Units/Apartments sold

In terms of residential property statistics for Victoria and Melbourne from 1987 - 2018 the following interactive stacked bar graph visualises the number Units/Apartments sold throughout this period. Additionally, the number of Units/Apartments sold are segregated by region, with Melbourne City and Country Victoria having the least number of houses sold every year. However, this trend in the number of Units/Apartments sold across Melbourne City is expected, as land is scarcer, hence, the number of developments and number of Units/Apartments sold would proportionally would be significantly lower relative to suburban areas. Additionally, the low number of houses sold across the countryside regions also isn't alarming, as contrary to land scarcity within Melbourne City, there's an abundance of land throughout the countryside with significantly lower demand. Hence, there's minimal development plans for rapid Unit/Apartments expansion and construction.
